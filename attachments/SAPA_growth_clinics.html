The SAPA Red Flag Alerts System

A clinic-customized service that extends your care into patients' homes, alerting both patients and doctors to potential health issues for timely intervention.

How It Works

  • 1 Share Your Link: Clinics receive a unique, branded link to share with patients via QR code or direct messaging.
  • 2 Patient Forms: Patients use simple forms for growth monitoring and 19 common conditions as advised or needed.
  • 3 Instant Alerts: If red flags are detected, the system immediately alerts the patient to visit the clinic.
  • 4 Doctor's Report: You receive a detailed report by email. Patient-identifiable information is auto-deleted for privacy.

What Problem Does It Solve?

The Challenge: Delayed Detection

Almost 70% of red flags are missed in clinics due to low parental awareness and doctors' focus on common conditions.

The Solution: Proactive Alerts

Our system bridges this gap by alerting both parents and doctors to potential issues early with embedded, context-specific education.

The Benefit: Superior Outcomes

Offer patients assurance of timely care, building trust and leading to superior health outcomes through early detection.
